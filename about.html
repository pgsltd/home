Our company

A construction partner focused on practical delivery.

Planning Gain Specialist Ltd is positioned to undertake a broad range of non-residential and residential construction work. This includes new building projects, rebuilding, remodelling, renovation, major refurbishment, structural alterations, extensions, conversions, maintenance and restoration.

The company can support property owners, businesses, developers, landlords, housing providers and organisations requiring coordinated building services. Depending on the project, the company may operate as the main contractor or contribute as a subcontractor within a wider construction programme.

Our capabilities

Supporting different properties, sectors and project requirements.

01 / COMMERCIAL

Commercial & Institutional Buildings

Offices, retail units, warehouses, workshops, factories, hotels, schools, healthcare premises, religious buildings and indoor sports facilities.

02 / RESIDENTIAL

Homes & Residential Developments

Detached, semi-detached and terraced houses, bungalows, flats, apartment buildings, housing-association and local-authority properties.

Major residential renovation, commercial refurbishment, restoration, internal remodelling and broader property modernisation.
